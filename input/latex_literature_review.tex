\section{Introduction}\label{sec:introduction}
LaTeX has long been a foundational tool for scientific and technical writing,
valued for its precision in mathematical typesetting and its separation of
content from presentation. This sample now doubles as a feature showcase for
\texttt{tex2pdf}: the citation to the general LaTeX overview \cite{latexwiki}
should jump to the bibliography entry, while the reference to
\autoref{fig:latex-logo} should jump to the figure itself in the generated PDF.

\section{Core Themes in the Literature}\label{sec:themes}
Two consistent themes appear in the literature: quality and control. Authors
emphasize that LaTeX produces stable, high-quality typography and gives writers
fine-grained control over structure and notation. A second theme is portability,
where source files are plain text and easy to version, compare, and reuse.
That portability is one reason cross-references such as the forward reference to
\autoref{sec:adoption} remain reliable even as a document evolves.

\section{Adoption in Academic Workflows}\label{sec:adoption}
Studies of writing practices note that LaTeX is frequently adopted in fields
with heavy mathematical content, such as physics, computer science, and
engineering. Its extensibility through packages also supports specialized
formats for journals and conferences, easing submission workflows. The logo in
\autoref{fig:latex-logo} is included here to show that the template can compile
images alongside ordinary text, labels, and citations.

\section{Challenges and Learning Curve}\label{sec:challenges}
While the benefits are substantial, reports also note the learning curve for
new users. Authors recommend gradual onboarding, templates, and clear
documentation to reduce initial friction. In practical terms, this sample also
shows that hyperlink-aware references can make navigation easier in longer PDFs.

\section{Conclusion}\label{sec:conclusion}
Overall, the literature presents LaTeX as a durable, standards-based platform
for technical communication, with trade-offs that can be mitigated through
instruction and tooling. As a template, this file now demonstrates images,
cross-references such as \autoref{sec:adoption}, and IEEE-style citations such
as \cite{latexwiki}.
